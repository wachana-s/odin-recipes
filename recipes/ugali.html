Ugali Recipe

Description

Ugali is a staple food and is also the main starch consumed in kenya. It is consumed by hand generally with some leafy greens or stew.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Maize flour
  • 1.5 cups of water
  • 3 tables spoons of salt for taste

Steps

  1. Place maize and water into pot.
  2. Put pan on stove, start mixing slowly until all water is gone.
  3. Keep mixing, turn over globule for 5 minutes.
  4. Plate.
